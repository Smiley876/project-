The Christmas Carol

Charles Dickens

"A Christmas Carol" by Charles Dickens is a novella about Ebenezer Scrooge, a miserly and cold-hearted old man. On Christmas Eve, Scrooge is visited by the ghost of his former business partner, Marley, who warns him of a similar fate if he doesn't change his ways. Throughout the night, Scrooge is visited by three spirits who show him the past, present,and future, leading to his transformation and newfound generosity

The Hobbit

by J.R.R. Tolkien

The Hobbit is a fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien, set in Middle-earth. It follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, an unassuming hobbit, who joins a group of dwarves led by the wizard Gandalf on a quest to reclaim a treasure guarded by the dragon Smaug
